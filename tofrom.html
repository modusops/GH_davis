    ToFrom by Rokitt

    Lead UX designer for An AI-driven travel booking app with aims to help simplify the way we search, book and travel. I was a part of the founding team and worked closely with the founders during the early seed stage. We came up short when the company ran out of funds and were unable to execute on a high quality product.

Personalization

The core concept of ToFrom, is the idea that booking for your next trip should be simple and easy. Searching is a hassle in most websites, offering the user too many search options and requiring the user to look hard. With the personalization feature, we ask users who they are upfront as those are conditions that most likely won't change.
